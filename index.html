THE ART

THE ARTIST

‘Replicating what I see as accurately as possible’ is Tanveer’s goal when it comes to his art work. Focussing on portraiture and strong contrast between light and dark with great detail, he enjoys the process of recreating real life on paper. Tanveer has always enjoyed art and drawing, throughout school he was encouraged to pursue the subject but was often disillusioned with the course curriculum and leaned towards understanding art for himself, where he developed a deep appreciation for Renaissance and Baroque art, in particular the works of Michelangelo, Caravaggio, and Bernini.


Despite this, Tanveer gave up art to pursue other subjects and a career in Finance, however, after discovering photorealism one afternoon, he was inspired to pick up a pencil and start drawing again and through months of practising and studying other artists techniques he is getting closer to achieving his goal of replicating real life on paper. A number of current photorealism artists inspire him to continue to draw and strive to improve. Tanveer hopes to continue along his journey whilst also adding more skills to his repertoire and also be an example to others that anyone and everyone can draw!
